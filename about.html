The Vault 93 Story

It started with a passion. In 2020, a group of automotive enthusiasts and diecast collectors came together with a simple vision: to create a premium destination where collectors could find the highest quality scale models from around the world in one reliable shop.

We noticed a gap in the market‚Äîcollectors were struggling to find authentic, limited-edition pieces from premium brands like Pop Race, Mini GT, and Hotwheels. The existing options were either unreliable or lacked the curation and expertise that serious collectors deserved.

Vault 93 was born. Named after the legendary year in automotive history and inspired by the concept of a secure vault protecting precious treasures, we set out to build more than just an online store. We created a community where passion meets precision, where every model tells a story, and where collectors can trust they're getting authentic pieces at fair prices.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of collectors worldwide, offering an ever-growing catalog of premium diecast models, exclusive releases, and hard-to-find pieces. But we're just getting started. Our mission remains the same: to be your trusted partner in building the ultimate collection.
